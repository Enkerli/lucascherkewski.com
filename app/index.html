I’m a designer, which means a bunch of things to me, but maybe different ones to you.

In any case, I’m a fan of the idea that design can be used to solve problems of all shapes and sizes. You can view some of the problems I’ve solved with design in my office, where I also explain just what it was that good design accomplished in each case.

If you’re here for a more personal side of me (though, to be clear, the work I do is very important to me, personally), you may want to read whatever I’m writing in my notepad, or about what I’m reading in my library.

Thanks for stopping by.
