Welcome to my home on the internet!

I’m a designer in Ottawa, just doing my best. I’m interested in how design can help humanity, and I work here to determine my part in that process, while also exploring my various interests.

This site is a holding grounds for things related to me: those I’ve worked on, and those I’ve cultivated. This site has a manifesto to suggest what a personal site could be.

P.S. I really do view this as my home on the internet. Feel free to come in and make yourself comfortable; you’re invited to explore these public workings of my brain and get to know me a bit better.

If anything here strikes you, don’t hesitate to email me. I look forward to hearing from you!
