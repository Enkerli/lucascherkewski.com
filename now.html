Now

I’m sharing my time between Ottawa and Waterloo.

I’m spending the next few months working on select client projects. I’m also dedicating time to community civic tech projects and some long-neglected personal projects. Here are some examples:

  • Collecting and summarizing federal government spending, to help citizens understand what and to who governments contract (an Ottawa Civic Tech project)
  • Returning to my website with new writing
  • Writing talks about research and working methods, and finding places to deliver them

To be frank, I’m mostly decompressing after constant busyness during the last few months of school. With my newly returned spare time, I’m exploring Ottawa and diving into non-academic interests. This means lots of reading, which you can track on Goodreads.

I’m also intentionally reconnecting with friends and overcoming my tendency toward social isolation. If you want to meet up, to reconnect or to get to know each other better, I’m happy to chat!
